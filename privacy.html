Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

1. Overview

STEP Quiz Uploader ("the App") is a personal content management tool for uploading educational quiz videos to social media platforms. This policy describes how the App handles data.

2. Data We Access

When you authorize the App, we access only:

  • Basic profile information — your user ID and display name, used to identify your account for uploading.
  • Content publishing permissions — to upload videos on your behalf.

3. Data We Store

The App stores authentication tokens locally on your device only. These tokens are used to maintain your login session and are never transmitted to any server other than the platform's official API (TikTok, Instagram, or YouTube).

4. Data We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, or share:

  • Personal information beyond basic profile data
  • Analytics or tracking data
  • Contact information
  • Location data
  • Financial information

5. Third-Party Services

The App connects to the following platforms via their official APIs:

  • TikTok — governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy
  • Instagram / Meta — governed by Instagram's Privacy Policy
  • YouTube / Google — governed by Google's Privacy Policy

6. Data Deletion

You can revoke the App's access at any time through your TikTok, Instagram, or YouTube account settings. Deleting the local token files (tiktok_token.json, instagram_token.json, token.json) removes all stored authentication data.

7. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
